Building bridges between art, technology, science and well-being

I'm Thiago Rossi Roque, a Postdoctoral Associate at the Neuroscience of Perception and Action Lab at the Italian Institute of Technology, in Rome, Italy, under the supervision of Dr. Giacomo Novembre. I am broadly interested in undertanding neural mechanisms of social interactions and social connection, mainly through creative and artistic practices such as music and dance. More specifically, I investigate how spontaneous joint actions result in coupled brain activity between individuals, how this inter-brain coupling is related to social connection, and what are the mechanisms underlying this phenomenon. I use a combination of behavioral paradigms, hyperscanning EEG, brain stimulation, and motion capture to investigate these questions. More details on my specific projects can be found on the Research page.

Background

I have a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering and a M.Sc. in Engineering degrees from University of Campinas (UNICAMP), Brazil. I've studied music composition (also at UNICAMP) for 4 years before receiving my Fulbright Scholarship and moving to the U.S.A. for my Ph.D. I spent 1.5 years in the Center for Music Technology at the Georgia Institute of Technology, where I started my Ph.D. research on the neuroscience of musical interactions, before moving to the University of Colorado, Boulder, where I concluded my Ph.D. at the ATLAS Institute with a triple major in Creative Technology and Design, Neuroscience, and Cognitive Neuroscience. With over 8 years of experience in hardware development, embedded systems and signal processing, I have been part of many projects involving analog audio processing, music information retrieval, IOT sensor networks and self-powered device. I have several publications in national and international congress proceedings, including presentations at AES Brazil, International Computer Music Conference (ICMC), Tangible and Embodied Interaction (TEI), and the European Society for Cognitive and Affective Neuroscience. As a musician, I have experience in composition and sound design for plays, videos and electronic games, as well as acting in various jazz, rock and brazilian country music group, playing keyboard or electric bass.
